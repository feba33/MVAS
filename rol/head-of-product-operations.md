# Head of Product Operations

## Definición

**Product Operations (Product Ops)** es la función que **habilita a la organización de Producto a escalar** mediante procesos, *tooling*, *insights*, planificación y *enablement*. El **Head of Product Operations** lidera esta función, reportando típicamente al **CPO / VP Product / Head of Product**, y actúa como *force multiplier* para Product Managers (PMs), Product Design, Engineering y *Go-to-Market* (GTM).

> **Analogía**: Si *Sales Ops* habilita a Ventas y *RevOps* alinea Ingresos, *Product Ops* habilita a **Producto** — la "máquina" que decide **qué** construir y **por qué**.

## Mandato y alcance

| Pilar | Responsabilidades del Head of Product Ops |
|-------|-------------------------------------------|
| **Procesos y Gobernanza** | Diseñar y mantener *Product Development Lifecycle (PDLC)*: *discovery → definition → delivery → launch → iteration*; *stage-gates*, *RACI*, *decision rights*, *rituales* (quarterly planning, sprint reviews, launch retrospectives) |
| **Tooling y *Stack* de Producto** | Seleccionar, implementar y gobernar: *Product Management Platform* (Jira Product Discovery, Productboard, Aha!, Craft.io, Linear, Shortcut), *Analytics* (Amplitude, Mixpanel, Heap, Pendo), *Feedback/VOC* (UserVoice, Canny, Productboard Portal), *Experimentation* (Statsig, LaunchDarkly, Optimizely, GrowthBook), *Roadmapping*, *Docs/Wiki* (Notion, Confluence, GitBook) |
| **Insights y *Voice of Customer (VoC)*** | Centralizar *feedback* (support tickets, sales calls, NPS/CSAT, interviews, analytics, community), *taxonomy* unificada, *insight repository* (Dovetail, EnjoyHQ, Condens, Aurelius), *recurring synthesis* (monthly/quarterly *Voice of Customer Report* para PMs y Leadership) |
| **Planificación y *Roadmap Operations*** | Facilitar *quarterly/annual planning*: *capacity modeling*, *dependency mapping*, *OKR alignment*, *roadmap tooling hygiene*, *portfolio view* (themes, investments, *health metrics*), *scenario planning* |
| **Enablement de PMs** | *Onboarding* (30/60/90 días), *playbooks* (discovery, definition, launch, experimentation, sunsetting), *templates* (PRD, RFC, launch plan, retro), *training* (discovery techniques, analytics, experimentation, writing), *career framework* support, *community of practice* |
| **Métricas y *Health* de Producto** | Definir y rastrear: *Product Health Metrics* (adoption, engagement, retention, churn, NPS, *feature usage depth*), *Process Metrics* (cycle time, *lead time for changes*, *deployment frequency*, *change failure rate* — DORA aplicado a producto), *Outcome Metrics* (OKR attainment, *business impact*) |
| **Launch / GTM Coordination** | *Launch calendar*, *launch tiers* (T1/T2/T3), *checklists*, *cross-functional coordination* (Marketing, Sales, CS, Support, Legal, Security, Docs), *post-launch tracking* (adoption, incidents, feedback) |
| **Comunicación y *Stakeholder Management*** | *Product updates* (internos/externos), *release notes*, *changelog*, *executive summaries*, *feedback loops* con Sales/CS/Marketing/Support, *product advisory council* (clientes clave) |
| **Experimentación y *Data Culture*** | *Experimentation platform* governance, *statistical rigor* (power analysis, *multiple testing correction*), *experiment review board*, *culture of learning* (failures = insights), *self-serve analytics* enablement |
| **Presupuesto y *Vendor Management*** | *Product stack budget*, *contract negotiations*, *renewals*, *ROI tracking* de herramientas, *consolidation* iniciativas |

## KPIs del Head of Product Ops

| Categoría | KPIs |
|-----------|------|
| **Velocidad y Eficiencia** | *Cycle time* (idea → launch), *lead time for changes*, *deployment frequency*, % *unplanned work*, *process adherence* (stage-gate compliance) |
| **Calidad de Decisiones** | % *decisions backed by data/insights*, *experiment velocity* (experimentos/mes/PM), *win rate* experimentos, *time to insight* (feedback → actionable insight) |
| **Salud del Producto** | *Feature adoption* (30/90 días), *usage depth* (DAU/MAU, *core actions*), *retention* (N-day, *bracket retention*), *NPS/CSAT* por feature, *churn attributable to product gaps* |
| **Enablement** | *PM onboarding time-to-productivity*, *playbook adoption rate*, *training completion*, *PM satisfaction* (eNPS interno), *community participation* |
| **Tooling** | *Tool adoption* (MAU/licencia), *stack cost per PM*, *tool consolidation savings*, *integration health* (uptime, data freshness) |
| **Launch** | *Launch on-time rate*, *launch tier mix*, *post-launch adoption* (T1), *incident rate* post-launch, *stakeholder satisfaction* |
| **Strategic Alignment** | *OKR attainment* (Product), *roadmap stability* (% changes per quarter), *capacity allocation* vs. *strategic themes*, *dependency resolution time* |

## Estructura típica del equipo (según escala)

| Escala | Headcount típico | Roles |
|--------|------------------|-------|
| **Startup (PMs 5-15)** | 1-2 | Head of Product Ops (generalista) + *Analytics/Insights Analyst* (compartido) |
| **Scale-up (PMs 15-50)** | 3-6 | Head, *Insights/VoC Lead*, *Process/Tooling Lead*, *Planning/Roadmap Ops*, *Enablement/Training*, *Analytics Analyst* |
| **Enterprise (PMs 50+)** | 8-20+ | **Director/VP Product Ops**, *Managers* por pilar (Insights, Process, Planning, Enablement, Launch, Analytics), *Analysts*, *Program Managers*, *Technical Writer* (docs), *Vendor Manager* |

## Diferenciación vs. roles afines

| Rol | Foco principal | Overlap | Diferencia clave |
|-----|----------------|---------|------------------|
| **Product Manager (PM)** | *Qué* construir y *por qué* (strategy, discovery, prioritization) | Usa procesos/tools de ProdOps | PM = *decision maker*; ProdOps = *enabler* (no decide *what*, decide *how we work*) |
| **Product Owner (PO)** | *Backlog management*, *sprint execution*, *stakeholder comms* táctico | Rituales ágiles | PO = *táctico/entrega*; ProdOps = *estratégico/escalabilidad* |
| **Program Manager (TPM/PgM)** | *Entrega cross-funcional*, *dependencies*, *timeline* | *Launch coordination*, *planning* | TPM = *project execution*; ProdOps = *product org capability* |
| **Product Analytics / Data Analyst** | *Análisis profundo*, *modelado*, *experiment design* | *Insights*, *metrics* | Analyst = *expert analysis*; ProdOps = *democratize insights*, *self-serve*, *process* |
| **Sales Ops / RevOps** | *Go-to-market efficiency*, *pipeline*, *quota* | *Launch coordination*, *feedback loops* | RevOps = *revenue engine*; ProdOps = *product engine* |
| **Engineering Manager / DevOps / Platform** | *Delivery técnico*, *infra*, *CI/CD*, *reliability* | *DORA metrics*, *tooling integration* | Eng = *how to build*; ProdOps = *what/why to build + org capability* |
| **Chief of Staff (Product)** | *Strategic projects*, *exec support*, *special initiatives* | *Planning*, *communication* | CoS = *ad hoc/strategic*; ProdOps = *ongoing/operational capability* |

## *Product Ops Maturity Model* (referencia)

| Nivel | Características |
|-------|-----------------|
| **1. Ad Hoc** | Procesos informales, tools dispersos (hojas de cálculo, Slack), *tribal knowledge*, *firefighting*, PMs pierden >50% tiempo en *ops* |
| **2. Emergente** | *Playbooks* básicos, *single source of truth* (roadmap tool), *weekly insights digest*, *launch checklist*, *onboarding* documentado |
| **3. Estructurado** | *PDLC* formal con *stage-gates*, *tool stack* integrado, *VoC taxonomy*, *quarterly planning process*, *PM enablement program*, *metrics dashboard* ejecutivo |
| **4. Optimizado** | *Self-serve analytics*, *automated governance* (policy-as-code), *predictive insights* (AI), *continuous planning*, *experimentation at scale*, *cross-functional launch engine* |
| **5. Transformador** | *Product Ops como plataforma*, *AI-augmented decision making*, *real-time portfolio optimization*, *ecosystem orchestration* (partners, marketplace) |

## Retos críticos 2025-2027

| Reto | Enfoque Head of Product Ops |
|------|----------------------------|
| **AI-augmented Product Development** | *AI-assisted discovery* (síntesis feedback, *opportunity scoring*), *AI-generated PRDs/specs*, *synthetic user testing*, *copilot para analytics* — *governance* de *AI en el loop* |
| **PLG / Self-Serve Motion** | *Product-led growth* requiere *instrumentación profunda*, *activation funnels*, *PQL definition*, *in-product experimentation* — *ProdOps owns the PLG engine* |
| **Multi-product / Portfolio Complexity** | *Platform vs. product line* tension, *shared services*, *dependency hell*, *investment allocation* — *Portfolio Ops* (themes, *capacity investment*, *strategic bets*) |
| **Tool Sprawl & Cost** | *Stack consolidation*, *build vs. buy*, *data integration* (CDP + Product Analytics + Warehouse), *vendor rationalization* |
| **Remote/Hybrid Product Teams** | *Async-first processes*, *digital collaboration*, *knowledge management*, *culture rituals* — *ProdOps designs the remote product culture* |
| **Regulatory / Compliance in Product** | *Privacy by design*, *AI Act conformity*, *accessibility*, *export controls* — *ProdOps embeds compliance gates in PDLC* |
| **Talent & PM Career Growth** | *PM career ladders*, *skill gaps* (discovery, analytics, strategy), *hiring/onboarding at scale* — *ProdOps partners with People/Head of Product* |
